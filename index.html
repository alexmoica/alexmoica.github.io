About Me

I am a second year software engineering co-op student at McMaster University in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada. I will be graduating April 2022 with a Bachelor of Engineering & Co-op degree. My main career interests are in software fields with a focus on machine learning, information security, and android development. Through my involvement in executive roles with McMaster's engineering society, I have also become highly proficient in all aspects of leadership - being able to lead and unite teams in identifying and resolving challenges of both technical and social nature, all while being accountable to the faculty, university, and provincial organizations.

Overall, I am open to learning about any topic in computing and can quickly pick up the essential skills and knowledge necessary for it. The personal projects that I am currently committed to are small in scope and mainly focused on learning and becoming efficient with the given language, IDE, or tool used in them. In the not-too-distant future I plan to shift my focus more on larger projects around global issues in technology and software. My interest in computing came from video games when I was young, developing into a curiosity about how they were made and created, from programming, to graphic design, to 3D modelling. After some research, I decided to try my hand at creating games of my own, ranging from simple Scratch and GameMaker games to more advanced Unity 2D and 3D ones.

This gave me a comfortable background in tools such as the Unity Engine, Adobe Photoshop, and Blender. After a while, this interest expanded to applications of all types, leading to where I am today. Outside of technical knowledge, I can speak Romanian as a second language and my hobbies include playing the guitar, Muay Thai, skateboarding, and reading; with some of my favourite authors being Isaac Asimov, Dan Brown, and Frank Herbert.

I hope you enjoy your experience on my website and do not hesitate to contact me for any questions or inquiries!
